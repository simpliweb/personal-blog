Trip to Dominican

It's about all-inclusive package

If you need to relax and look for affordable place to go on a vacation. I'd highly recommend spending time looking for all-inclusive packages.
Domincan republic was a perfect get away. No need to worry about food width almost all-day buffet. Unlimited alcohol and the beach was less than 3min walk from our suite.

Eat, sleep, drink, repeat

There are also a lot of activities within the resort which is extra. However if you are like me, who likes to do nothing but eat or drink and sleep. You can do that all day long for 7 days, it was so great that it felt like it was only 3 day trip!
