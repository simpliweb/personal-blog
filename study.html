Student again

Deciding to change my career after 6 years, was not the easiest decision. I've always felt that I wanted to do something different, yet I got too comfortable at my previous job.
I want to share that it is never too late to study and work at a field that you see your future with. Scroll down to find out how I got my hands on coding!

Coursera

Studying for a new career can be very freightening. To get myself started right, I researched through the web and asked close friends for advice. I found out that there are lots of opportunities in the IT world. I also thought about studying coding while I was working, but I was just too hesitent and did not think fast.
Coursera is one of the online course platform where you can take courses for free by choosing 'audit' option, and or pay for a certification from recognized Universities.
I loved this online school because I am not competing anyone, and totally self-paced.
