Trip to Vancouver

Best places to eat

Vancouver has better choice in some type of East Asian food compared to Toronto. Better in taste and variety. Food is one of the big reasons why I love travelling to Vancouver. If you want some quality yet affordable food. Here are some of my recommendations:

  • Yamato Sushi: 616 Davie Street, Vancouver
  • Tsui Hang Village Restaurant: 1193 Granville Street, Vancouver
  • Laksa King: 2546 E Hastings Street, Vancouver
Scenerey is key to destress

Moving away from crazy city life in Toronto, get away to smaller cities in Vancouver. Even just for 3-4day weekends, will definitely be enough to recharge. Definitely recommend hiking, and there are so many great places to bike as well.
